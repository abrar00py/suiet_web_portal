About INSTITUTE OF ENGINEERING AND TECHNOLOGY

The Institute of Engineering & Technology is a constituent college under Srinivas University started with offering 4 years Bachelor Degree program B.Tech. in Civil Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Computer Science Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Robotics, Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Cyber Security & Cyber Forensics, Data Science, Cloud Technology & Information Security and Block Chain Technology & Distributed Computing . The college offers 2 years Master Degree program M.Tech in Structural Engineering, Construction Management, Computer Science & Engineering, Industrial Nano- Biotechnology, and Artificial Intelligence & Virtual Reality. The college also offers doctoral program in all the steams leading to Ph.D degree. The college is located at Srinivas University Campus, Mukka, Mangaluru.


The Institute of Engineering & Technology was established with the aim of providing international standards in education and training and hence to prepare graduates with Knowledge and Skills in the emerging fields of Engineering. It opens knowledge by immersing students in a rigorous and innovative curriculum and culture of real-world, experience-based learning. The Institution has a continually evolving infrastructure development programme which quickly adapts to the challenges of the new evolution in engineering technology. Located in the sylvan surroundings and cool climes of picturesque Srinivas University Campus at Mukka and abets the National Highway 66. The college campus is developed in a lush green environment with well qualified & experienced faculty, state-of-the-art laboratories and infrastructural facilities, high speed wi-fi internet connection and digital library facilities with the aim of providing Technical Education, Professional Training, Soft Skill Training and Research facilities in the field of Engineering, Technology and Management Science as per International Standards with the aim of creating Technically Sound Engineers and Decision Makers who can establish themselves successfully in the industry and global market. The institute has hi-tech gallery class rooms with latest teaching aids such as LCD projectors, audio system and multimedia facilities to train the students by implementing e -learning and teaching environment. The institute aims to produce career – ready graduates who are immediately employable, self-reliant and ethical. It has been the endeavour of the college to provide a healthy educational environment that fosters innovation and enthusiasm in students. Having well established facilities and amenities, the College of Engineering & Technology is on the threshold of a quantum leap.
